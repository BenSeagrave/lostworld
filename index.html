About Us

Get to know Lost World

We’re a small new shop founded in 2020, located in Harlow, Essex, with a team dedicated to providing you with your perfect tattoo. Whether it’s black and grey, colour, realistic or traditional, we do it all, and each artist has their own style so you can choose who is best suited to your needs.

We offer a free design service where applicable and can offer knowledgable advice to help you make the right decision. For us, a friendly and welcoming environment is just as important as our art, so whether it’s with a hot drink, putting a film on, or simply a chat we strive to make you feel as comfortable as possible.

If this sounds like the tattoo experience you’re after, then pop down and get booked in. We look forward to seeing you!

COVID FRIENDLY

Due to the clean nature of our practice, we nearly meet the COVID measures as standard, but we have taken extra precautions to keep everyone safe. We therefore ask you to wear a mask for your whole appointment, and bring no guests, to limit the amount of people in the studio.

CUSTOM TATTOO PIECES

All pieces are custom designed to suit your style, whether you have a specific image you like or just an idea , we can work with ideas thrown at us.

CLEAN & HYGIENIC

Everything is deep cleaned before and after every tattoo, and we use disposable needles and tubes every time. Hygiene is the most important thing to us, and this will be noticeable when you come in for your session.

Contact

Get in touch

For any queries please contact our shop where we have a dedicated team to helping you with any questions you may have. Our artists are very busy so if you want to talk to them directly you can book a one on one consultation with them.

There is free parking right outside the shop, but no cash machine so please come prepared if you have an appointment or wish to place a deposit down.
